Welcome to the Aim Feedback Experiment

Thank you for volunteering for this study. You will complete a short reaction-time task where you click on targets that appear on the screen. The task involves different types of feedback (auditory or visual) that will be assigned randomly. You will first answer a few background questions, practice the task, complete three 30 second trials, and finish with a brief questionnaire. The whole process should take less than 10 minutes.

Your responses and performance will be recorded anonymously. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. By checking the box below and entering your name, you indicate that you have read and understood the information and consent to participate.

Note: This experiment may require auditory feedback. Please ensure you are in a quiet environment and have access to headphones or speakers.
